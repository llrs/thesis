1.4 Relationship between samples

Each sample has the correspondent sample on the other data-set or not, so if for the same patient we can measure the transcriptomics, the metabolomics and the methylation at the same time it is XXXX. Sometimes because the piece of the patient obtained through surgery is not big enough to be divided into the different experiments needed. In some cases we have transcriptomics data from some patients and microbiom data of other patients. This results in a new source of variation that has to be dealt with. Which complicates the conclusion one can draw from the studies of these kind of data.
