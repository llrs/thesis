Chapter 2 RGCCA

The regularized generalized canonical correlation is a method that combines several datasets, using data from the same sample, that is appropiate when the number of variables is higher than samples.

(Tenenhaus and Hanafi 2010; Tenenhaus 2008; A. Tenenhaus, Philippe, and Frouin 2015; A. Tenenhaus and Tenenhaus 2011; A. Tenenhaus and Tenenhaus 2014; A. Tenenhaus et al. 2014)
